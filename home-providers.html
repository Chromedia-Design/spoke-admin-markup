Introducing SurgeryHub™

from Spoke Health™

We reduce the cost of employer-provided health care by serving their employees' greatest needs during the most expensive time in their medical lives.

Proactive Engagement

Through a proprietary system, SurgeryHub™ reaches patients and engages them at the moment when they are most likely to be making a decision about a surgical procedure. This proactive approach provides the greatest opportunity to convert a potential patient into a booked patient.

Risk-Free Billing

We take the risk out of payment for you.

By pre-paying for a booked procedure, we remove the risk in collection from insurance, and out-of-pocket from patients. This also eliminates the up to 90-day wait in collecting outstanding claims from insurance, and allows you to focus more of your time and resources on providing top-notch service to your patients.

Patient Access

Our system selects facilities based on the greatest value to each individual patient.

This means that we will be providing patients to you that come not only from your local area that would previously have been unreachable, but from all over the country. Your quality puts you at the top of your field, and you deserve to share that expertise as much as possible.

Who We Work With

By focusing on only facilities with the highest quality, determined through quantifiable outcome, complication, readmission and other metrics, we provide our clients with the confidence to select a facility knowing they will have a positive experience. And by continually driving them to consider quality from our very first contact, we create patients that recognize that options exist, and considering you is in their best interest.

Teamwork

Every patient that comes through the Spoke system will be partnered with a dedicated concierge. This is an individual that will assist both you and the patient in ironing out all of the details and making sure the patient is completing all of their pre and post surgical checklists to ensure the best possible outcome.

At Spoke Health™, our mission is to substantially reduce the cost of healthcare for employers and their employees without sacrificing the quality of care by providing transparency and access to the best cost and quality surgical facilities and surgeons, and guiding employees and their families through a frictionless, stress-free surgery experience.
