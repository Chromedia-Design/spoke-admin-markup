COVID-19 Screening Tool and Clinical Response Solution

This tool provides access to a screening questionnaire and will present you the best guidelines as provided by the Proactive MD. For persons with symptoms you will be provided with private and secure chat or video conference (telehealth) connections to local clinical responders who will recommend next steps.

COVID-19 Response System

1

Register an account so we can communicate information about your health in a safe and secure environment.

2

Answer some questions about your symptoms, travel, and the contact you’ve had with others.

3

Receive DOH approved guidance on your next steps.

4

If necessary, chat with a local healthcare responder who may schedule a video call with a doctor from the comfort and safety of your home. No need to go to a clinic or hospital unless directed to.

Your Privacy Matters

Spoke Health complies with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) for all information collected. Your information will be shared with healthcare professionals. Your data will NOT be shared with any person or organization outside of the COVID-19 response team.
