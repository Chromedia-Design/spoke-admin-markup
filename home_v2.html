An innovative value-based surgical care & patient concierge program for insurers and health-plan sponsors.

A great solution for insurance and benefits management companies, health-plan sponsors and TPA’s.

The cost of surgeries is the largest segment of healthcare spend (over 1/3 of total cost to commercial payers).

The estimated overspend on surgeries by commercial payers is over $75 billion a year and growing rapidly.

Unless they reduce the cost of surgeries, they will continue to struggle with controlling their total spend.

How Spoke Solves This

Spoke has developed a program and the required technology that allows us to serve the needs of all constituents. All supported by technology that is generations ahead of the current market.
