Spoke Health™ Launches New Provider Program

  Download PDF Document
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
DENVER, Colorado, March 24, 2017
Contact: Richard Coyte, richard@spokehealth.com

Spoke Health™ today announced the details of its new provider-affiliate program. Spoke gives a limited number of the highest rated local providers access to employees through non-negotiated, flexible arrangements.

Spoke’s role is to facilitate a patient-provider transaction based on each party's greatest needs when surgical intervention is required, matching supply with demand.

Spoke Health CEO, Richard Coyte, explained the goal of the program’s design:

We designed our program to serve providers as much as anyone.

We go direct to providers, eliminating the cost of supporting the out-of-control profit goals of insurance companies. Some people might not know that self-insured employers ‘rent’ provider networks, which means employees, employers and providers all pay the insurance companies’ in one way or another. By going directly to providers, we offer client-employees access to the best possible care; the providers access to prescreened patients; and savings to employers through competitive bundled case rates.

What we’ve learned is that by serving the needs of both patients and providers, we generate value on both sides of the transaction.

— Richard Coyte, Spoke Health™ CEO

That flexible arrangement gives the highest-rated local providers an opportunity to generate highly efficient revenue, optimize utilization and maximize their return on investment.

Key benefits to providers include:

  • a patient supported by an experienced care navigator;
  • payment in advance of surgery;
  • prescreened patients (reducing the risk of complications);
  • eliminating the cost of working with traditional payers; and
  • increased utilization based on the provider’s schedule.

If you would like to learn more about Spoke’s provider program and how to join, please contact Richard Coyte at richard@spokehealth.com.

About Spoke Health™

Spoke Health™ is a Denver-based, tech-enabled healthcare services company specializing in reducing the cost of surgeries for self-insured employers and third-party administrators. Its core product, SurgeryHub™, identifies employees likely on a path to surgical intervention and assigns a single, dedicated care navigator who personally reaches out to the employee, beginning the engagement and support process. At that moment, Spoke offers its affiliate providers access to the patient, matching supply with demand. The responding providers are presented to the patient in a sophisticated online consumer environment, offering employees financial rewards for making quality-and price-conscious surgery decisions.
