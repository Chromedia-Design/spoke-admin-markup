COVID-19 Screening Tool and Clinical Response Solution

This tool provides access to a screening questionnaire and will present you the best guidelines as provided by the Philippine Department of Health. For persons with symptoms you will be provided with private and secure chat or video conference (telehealth) connections to local clinical responders who will recommend next steps.

Zip complies with the Philippine Data Privacy Act (DPA) for all information collected. Your information will be shared with healthcare professionals, the Department of Health and will be used to compile statistics about the spread of the virus. Your data will NOT be shared with any person or organization outside of the COVID-19 response team.
