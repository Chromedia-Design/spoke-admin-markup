 OVERVIEW

Welcome to Precision Spine Center, a multidisciplinary treatment center of excellence serving Denver and the Rocky Mountain region. We are pioneers in the use of advanced 3D navigation during surgery to precisely target—and effectively treat— a wide variety of spinal disorders.

Our center is one of a select few centers in the U.S. to consistently use advanced 3D technology to address spine pathology with laser-guided precision.

Our Philosophy

At Precision Spine Center, surgery is not always the answer to our patient’s spinal issues. Our multi-disciplinary approach allows us to explore a variety of treatment options, depending upon the severity of the patient’s problems and pain.

We believe that open and timely communication with our patients and our referring physicians is critical in having an efficient and effective patient care model. In order to understand, we must communicate.

About Our Practice

The professionals at Precision Spine Center represent a multidisciplinary team of spine care experts with an excellent track record of success in treating a wide variety of spinal disorders. We are one of a very few centers in the United States to consistently use 3D navigation during surgery, as well as computer-assisted spine surgery. This advanced technology allows us to accurately treat the affected area without damaging healthy bone, tissue or nerves, and enables the precise placement of implanted devices to achieve optimal outcomes.

Precision Spine Center takes a truly patient-centric approach to healthcare. Our Physicians take the time to discuss the details of your medical condition and the various treatment/surgical options that are available. Precision Spine Center is a place where Physicians listen.

Spine care is our specialty, our sole focus, our passion. If you’re seeking long-term relief and/or optimum spine health, we invite you to consult with us.

When surgical intervention is the best option for your condition, you can be confident that our spine surgeons are among the region’s most skilled and capable. We employ the latest neurosurgical techniques.
