Overview

At the Florida Hospital Orlando, our primary goal is to cure every patient's cancer. Since we know that in many cases advanced stage cancers cannot be cured, our next goal is to make cancer a chronic disease, with which our patients can live and lead relatively normal lives.

We accomplish our goals daily in our Florida facility, which provides state-of-the-art, cutting edge medical treatment, and caring attention to the mind, body and soul of each and every patient. With this fusion of science and caring, we find that we are able to provide our patients with the best possible healing, and among the best cancer treatment available in Florida, and the world.

State-of-the-art medicine. State of mind healing.

Your comfort and care are most important to our physicians, nurses, imaging specialists, technicians, integrative medicine staff and support staff of the Beverly Hills Cancer Center. Our group is dedicated to providing our patients with the best cancer treatment available in Los Angeles and across the globe.

Our Center includes all of the facilities offered by a major hospital in one convenient location with an environment that features spacious, comfortable and private treatment rooms, infused with color and natural light. We are determined to provide unparalleled care, maintaining high-quality state-of-the-art treatment and dedicated clinical research while providing comfort and sustenance to the whole patient, in a healing and supportive environment.

We move very quickly to help you heal.

In cancer treatment, time is of the essence. Waiting a month, a week or even a day to begin treatment can mean the difference between success and failure. That's why the Beverly Hills Cancer Center provides complete diagnostic and treatment services, in a single facility and joined by a single, state-of-the-art medical records system ensuring you get the best, and most rapid cancer treatment available in Los Angeles. Your doctor will have your test results immediately, not in a week. And your treatment can begin right away.

"We are committed to providing complete care, which to us is the integration of high-quality, state-of-the-art treatment and compassionate personal human interaction," says Dr. A. Eli Gabayan. "The center's goal is to pursue total health and wellness by combining leading-edge technology, breakthrough research, and a variety of treatment modalities, while comforting the body, mind and soul in a healing, supportive, and interpersonal environment."

Complete diagnostic and treatment services in one convenient location.

When you visit our convenient Beverly Hills location, you will find any form of treatment you could need, not only for the condition that brought you here, but also your overall wellness.
