At the Florida Hospital Orlando, our primary goal is to cure every patient's cancer. Since we know that in many cases advanced stage cancers cannot be cured, our next goal is to make cancer a chronic disease, with which our patients can live and lead relatively normal lives. We accomplish our goals daily in our Florida facility, which provides state-of-the-art, cutting edge medical treatment, and caring attention to the mind, body and soul of each and every patient. With this fusion of science and caring, we find that we are able to provide our patients with the best possible healing, and among the best cancer treatment available in Florida, and the world.
Your comfort and care are most important to our physicians, nurses, imaging specialists, technicians, integrative medicine staff and support staff of the Beverly Hills Cancer Center. Our group is dedicated to providing our patients with the best cancer treatment available in Los Angeles and across the globe. Our Center includes all of the facilities offered by a major hospital in one convenient location with an environment that features spacious, comfortable and private treatment rooms, infused with color and natural light. We are determined to provide unparalleled care, maintaining high-quality state-of-the-art treatment and dedicated clinical research while providing comfort and sustenance to the whole patient, in a healing and supportive environment.
